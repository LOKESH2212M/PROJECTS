What is Numeracy ?

Numeracy is primarily the capability to use, explicate and communicate mathematical information to solve real-world problems. It is essentially important to perform everyday job functions.

Our Growing need for Numeracy

Evidence shows us that a positive attitude to maths is key to being numerate; having the confidence and competence to use numbers and think mathematically in everyday life. We use numeracy every day in all areas of our lives. Our confidence and ability with numbers impacts us financially, socially, and professionally.

Low wages

There is a direct relationship between wage distribution and numeracy skills. It undermines the economic performance of the country.
